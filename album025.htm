Anonymous Camera # 4:

These photos are from one of ten "throw-away" cameras that Ralph provided to attendees at the Madame Tussaud's party on Saturday night. Part of the normal photo processing was the conversion of each roll of film, to a Kodak Photo CD. These images were downconverted from those Photo Cds.
