Speaker Biography:
.

Mike Hurlston
Vice President, Marketing, Oren Semiconductors
Santa Clara, Ca.

Michael Hurlston, Vice President, Marketing at Oren Semiconductors, has over 11 years of semiconductor sales and marketing experience. He has been involved in several semiconductor start ups, most recently Oren Semiconductor. Mr. Hurlston joined Oren in 1997 and, together with two partners, formed the US headquarters of the company. As Vice President of Marketing at Oren, he was responsible for initiating the company’s successful development of digital television demodulators and its move into the fixed wireless market.

From 1995 to 1997, Mr. Hurlston was Director of Marketing at IC WORKS and headed the company’s spread spectrum clocking business unit. Prior to that, he held a variety of marketing and engineering positions at Exar and ICS. Mr. Hurlston has a BSEE, MSEE, and MBA from UC Davis.

Since the PBS Technology Conference, Mr. Hurlston has become Vice President of Marketing at Radia Communications.
