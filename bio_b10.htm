Speaker Biography:
.

Gregory Forbes
Transmission Systems engineer , PBS
Alexandria, Va.

Gregory currently serves as one of the two engineers at PBS, keeping the encoding, compression, and transmission systems up to date. Also researches DTV equipment and services. Serves in various ATSC T3 and IS/S3 subcommittees, as well as a long sentence in the field truck for the MSTV/NAB field tests in 2000.

He has worked with PBS for almost 4 years, and holds a BSEE from the University of Maryland (1992), and a MSEE from Virginia Tech (1998).
