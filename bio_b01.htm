Speaker Biography:
.

Dan Dickey
Director of Research and Development, ADC Broadcast Systems Division
McMurray, Pa.

Dan received his bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from the University of Missouri. He was infected with the broadcasting virus in 1975 and has been actively involved in the business ever since.

Dan has been responsible for designing several different types of transmitters ranging in frequency from 15kHz to 1.5Ghz at various power levels from 50W to 600kW. His current focus is mainly on research and development of high power transmitters for digital terrestrial television.

Dan is a member of the IEEE and in his spare time enjoys cycling and scuba diving.
