Gary Blievernicht
Director of Engineering, WKAR-TV / FM, Michigan State University
East Lansing, Mi.

Gary Blievernicht is Director of Engineering for the WKAR radio and television stations at Michigan State University. He has a history almost as long as Bruce Jacob's in public television, but with more hair loss. Eighteen years in PTV include 2 at KNME, 7 with EEN/IPS/APS/APTV, and now 9 with MSU. Gary has been the University's representative to Internet-2, and is a current member of the PBS Engineering Committee.

Previous experience includes nine years in commercial television and even a couple with RCA making CED video disks.
