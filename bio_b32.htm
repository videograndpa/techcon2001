Speaker Biography:
.

Bill Hendler
Vice President, Interactive Division, Chyron

Bill Hendler is VP of Interactive TV at Chyron Corporation. He joined Chyron in 1979 and has held positions in Engineering, Product Development, Sales, and Business Development. Bill has a broad-based knowledge in the field of broadcast technology, and he is the driving force responsible for channeling Chyron expertise to create unique solutions for ITV deployment and content creation. Considered one of the leading experts on the subjects of broadcast graphics technology and Interactive Television, Bill is a regular speaker at industry events.
