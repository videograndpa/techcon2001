Speaker Biography:
.

Marc Duvivier
Vice President, Technology, Wavexpress
New York, NY

Mr. Duvivier is in charge of Wavexpress' engineering team responsible for client technology, operational MIS, network transport, and service integration. His background includes more than 15 years of computer industry experience in a broad range of technology capacities, notably in the design and operation of high availability transaction processing systems. He founded the technology consulting firm Salliance, has extensive experience in project management, design, and operations, and was instrumental in the development of core processing for several Fortune 100 firms such as J.P. Morgan.

His experience also includes technology projects for firms such as Morgan Stanley and Sybase.
