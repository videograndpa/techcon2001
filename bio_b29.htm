Speaker Biography:
.

Scot Boyd
Program Manager and Technical Evangelist, Microsoft
Redmond, WA

Scot Boyd has worked for Microsoft for 6 years on technologies such as COM, DCOM, WMI, OLE Automation, .NET, and SOAP. He is currently the Program Manager and Technical Evangelist for the Microsoft TV Tools Program, which is focused on helping vendors make tools to create, manage, and broadcast ATVEF content.
