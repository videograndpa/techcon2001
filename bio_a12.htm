Speaker Biography:
.

Paul Shapiro
Senior Technologist, PBS Interactive
Alexandria, Va.

Paul Shapiro joined PBS Interactive in Septmber of 2000 to lead the implemetation of a Content Management System.

Paul holds a BS degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Buffalo. Prior to joining PBS, he spent several years supporting the Navy Systems Command Standardization Office in the development and maintenance of document management systems.
