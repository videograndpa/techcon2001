.

Tony Myers
Senior Manager, Information Technology, CPB
Washington, D.C.

Tony Myers is a Manager of Information Technology at the Corporation for Public Broadcasting. Heis involved in strategic technology planning, IT budgeting and operations management. He has also been heavily involved in the development of the Stations Grants Management System (SGMS), an online system that stations will use to file their financial reports with CPB.

Before joining CPB, Tony was a management and technology consultant at Krajewski and Associates, were he managed non-profit client accounts. As a consultant, Tony worked with clients on various projects, including strategic technology planning and systems selection and implementation. Other client activities included developing plans for integrating Web technologies with business unit operations such as fundraising, product sales and distribution and membership development. Tony has also worked as an IT manager for a higher education association and an international philanthropy. He has over ten years experience in IT management, budgeting and systems planning and implementations.
