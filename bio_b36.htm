Speaker Biography:
.

Ken Winston
Director, Network Technologoes, Thirteen / WNET
New York, NY.

Ken has been with Thirteen/WNET New York for three years. Along with his staff of twenty IT and database professionals, Ken is in charge for Thirteen's network of over 800 users. Ken is responsible for all IT and convergent broadcast application technologies. Ken was also the technical lead of Thirteen's move to a new state-of-the-art facility a year and a half ago.

After hours, Ken is an instructor and faculty member of the Computer Technologies and Applications Program at Columbia University in New York City, where -- apart from being an award-winning instructor he is the Chairman of the Network Administration and Design track.

Ken is both a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer and Microsoft Certified Trainer.
