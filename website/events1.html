Saturday Evening

The Saturday evening social event this year will be a private party at Madame Tussaud's Celebrity Encounter at The Venetian, a $20 million, two-story exhibit (one of just five world-wide) that opened last July. It features five themed environments showcasing more than 100 of the world's most popular film, television, music and sport celebrities, as well as legendary Las Vegas icons. Rub shoulders with gaming legend Bugsy Siegel, Hollywood icons Marilyn Monroe and Elizabeth Taylor; and music stars Wayne Newton, Tom Jones and Liberace. There's Elvis too, of course.

We'll have three hours to roam the rooms. You can get up-close-and-personal with all of them. (Bring your camera!) The only thing you can't get is autographs.
