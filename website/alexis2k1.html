As the annual Spring Engineering Conference evolved into the Spring Technology Conference, we found that the Thomas and Mack facilities, our "home" in Las Vegas for several years, could not accomodate our growing group. In 1998, we moved the event to the Alexis Park Resort Hotel.

The Conference registration area is located to the right of the lobby. The Apollo Ballroom, where the combined Conference sessions will take place, is located directly behind the registration area. The Athena Meeting Room, where the concurrent Information Technology sessions will meet, is nearby.


The Friday night party will be held poolside, and there will be various technology displays in the hallways, for you to view during session breaks.

If you are driving from another hotel, note that there is a larger parking area on the east side of the building than on the west. Parking spaces closest to the buildings are usually used by the hotel guests. With the number of hotel guests with cars, and the size of this Conference, parking particularly as more broadcasters arrive on Sunday, may become a problem.
