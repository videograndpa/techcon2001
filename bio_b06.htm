Speaker Biography:
.

Jerry Berger
Vice President, Marketing, AgileVision
Princeton, N.J.

Jerry Berger has more than 25 years experience in the electronics, broadcasting and post production industries. Prior to joining AgileVision as VP of Marketing, Mr. Berger was General Manager for the automation and transmission division for Sony Electronics, Broadcast and Professional group. There, he was responsible for marketing all of Sony's Video File Servers, Routing Switchers & MPEG 2 CODECs. Mr. Berger also held senior management posts in Sony's Industrial Camera division and the Broadcast and Distribution sales channels.

Prior to joining Sony in 1990, Mr. Berger was responsible for post-production operations at NBC Television Network, New York, and Modern VideoFilm - and A.M.E. - Hollywood.

Mr. Berger holds a Bachelor of Science degree from The City University of New York in Film and Television Production.
