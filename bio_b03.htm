Speaker Biography:
.

George Anderson
Manager of Digital Services, Thomcast Communications, Inc.
Southwick Ma.

George F. Anderson, III is Manager of Digital Services of the THOMCAST Communications Broadcast and Multimedia Division.

Mr. Anderson started at THOMCAST in January 2000 as Sales Manager for CDS with responsibility to promotion and sales of all the digital services necessary to interface a station's existing NTSC master control to the high definition master control, SD and HD encoding and datacasting capability, as well as interfacing to the station transmitter link distribution systems.

Mr. Anderson has many years experience in broadcast sales. He has provided sales consultation to major customers in traditional markets, and federal, state and county government agencies. His experience also includes media management, the movement of media both inter and intra-facility, and providing media management solutions for the emerging digital media market previously known as broadcast television.

Mr. Anderson holds a degree in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Marketing from the University of Alabama. He is the Section Chair of the Philadelphia Section of the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers, and was Eastern Region Governor in 1999.
