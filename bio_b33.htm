Speaker Biography:
.

Nandhu Nandhakumar
V.P. Engineering & CTO, Triveni Digital, Inc
Princeton Junction, NJ

Nandhu Nandhakumar is currently Vice President of Engineering and Chief Technology Office at Triveni Digital, where he manages engineering teams that develop, manufacture, integrate and support products for data enhanced digital television. He has a Masters degree from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and a PhD degree from the University of Texas at Austin in electrical and computer engineering.

Prior to Joining Triveni, he served on the faculties of UT Austin and the University of Virginia, Charlottesville, where he conducted research in video and image processing. Abandoning academia, and as director of the image and video processing group of LG Electronics Research Center, he led advanced technology development activities related to networked multimedia appliances, and MPEG-4 and MPEG-7 standards activities.
