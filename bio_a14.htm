Jeremy Roberts
Broadband and Streaming Media Evangelist, PBS Interactive
Alexandria, Va.

Jeremy Roberts has been with PBS Interactive for a little over a year now. Officially titled "Broadband and Streaming Media Evangelist", Jeremy has worked to develop the streaming offerings at pbs.org, including the searchable video sites such as Online NewsHour, Julia Child: Lessons with Master Chefs, Scientific American Frontier, Mathline, as well as others launched, or in the works.

In addition to his streaming media concentrations Jeremy has worked to develop and launch the Wireless PBS channel on AvantGo, and supports the Interactive Television efforts.
